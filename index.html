Why coffee is an awesome drink!

This website is dedicated to coffee. It contains information and pictures about coffee, the wonderful drink that keeps the world moving.

Get to know coffee.

Coffee beans, where the process begins. Beans are roasted, which gives them the flavour and aroma coffee is known for. Different roasting methods change the flavour of the end product.

Water temperature is essential in the brewing process because it affects the rate of extraction. Extraction refers to the flavors and substances that are dissolved from the coffee beans into your cup.

There are a multitude of ways to enjoy coffee. Cappuccino, latte, espresso or cold brew. These are just a few of the varieties in which to enjoy a brew.

Coffee serves as more than just a drink; it's a social activity that unites people. Historically, coffee houses have been an important social gathering point and their appearance encouraged several cultural and political transformations.
